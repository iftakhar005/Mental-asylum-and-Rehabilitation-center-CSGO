Four Stages of Cyber Attack

1. Infiltration

Initial unauthorized access to the system through various attack vectors

2. Propagation

Lateral movement and privilege escalation within the compromised system

3. Aggregation

Collection and consolidation of sensitive data for extraction

4. Exfiltration

Unauthorized transfer of collected data to external locations
